DELIVERY METHOD
Orders placed on our website are processed as follows:

PRINT: Newspapers for subscriptions to our print version are published twice a week on Mondays and Wednesdays and are delivered by pre-sorted second class mail via the United States Postal Service (USPS).

Newspaper(s) initiating a paid subscription which were completed online at www.cdcnews.com will be mailed to the mailing address provided during the online order and checkout process on the next available label printing date. Second class mailing labels are printed on the Thursday before a Monday publish date and on Monday for a Wednesday publish date. Newspapers are published on Tuesdays and Thursdays during weeks which have any Monday USPS postal holidays. Newspapers are only published on Mondays during the weeks of Thanksgiving and December religious holidays.

ONLINE ORDERS: Subscriptions including our Lead Manager online product are activated immediately upon credit card verification and authorization. An email containing the login information is sent via email to the email address provided during the online order and checkout process. Non-receipt of the email containing the Lead Manager login information due to personal or network spam-blockers and/or firewalls or any other unforeseen email blocking software is beyond the control of CDC Publishing LLC.

Project information in Lead Manager is added and updated daily, Monday through Friday, and is accessible using a valid login ID and password 24-hours a day / 7-days a week at www.cdcnews.com from any computer with internet access.
