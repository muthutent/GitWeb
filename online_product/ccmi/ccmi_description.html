Commercial Construction Market Intelligence™(CCMI™) is CDCNews newest addition to our award-winning lead service, Lead Manager. A new product that answers two of our customers most frequently asked questions:
  1. What have my competitors been bidding on and why do I lose out to them?
  2. Where else can I look to grow my business?
It is comprised of two search and reporting tools:

CCMI™-Companies allows you to search CDCNews extensive bidding archives and report bid results by company including times bid, bid results, types and sizes of projects bid in a selected timeframe.

CCMI™-Geographies allows you to search CDCNews bidding archives by geographies (states, or specific counties) and report bid results by various project characteristics, such as construction types, specific industries and types of work, within a selected timeframe.
